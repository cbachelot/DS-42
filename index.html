DS42 Overview

The ultimate answer to a consistent and scalable design language, user experience, and digital identity is... Design System 42.

We challenge ourselves every day to make consistent usefull designs that help make our users lives better. We acknowledge that this is a constant, moving goal that we must adapt to over time and we accept this challenge as a way to strive to be better tomorrow than we are today.
