Components

Overview

The components makeup the parts and elements of the visual aspect of our front-end applications. By ulitlizing these appropriately we will make visually consistent applications for our users.

Buttons

Buttons communicate the action that will occur when the user touches them.

Component buttons trigger a link reaction on press. They may display text, iconography, or both. Flat buttons and raised buttons are the most commonly used types.

Button Types

The most common button types are:

Flat buttons

Flat buttons are text-only buttons. They may be used in dialogs, toolbars, or inline. They do not lift, but fill with color on press.

Raised buttons

Raised buttons are rectangular-shaped buttons. They may be used inline. They lift and display reactions on press.

Elevation

Flat buttons: 0px

Raised buttons: 1px

Min Height: 36px
Left padding: 16px

Raised Button
Min Height: 36px

Right padding: 16px

Ease: .3s on hover, .1s on action

Secondary Buttons

The secondary button is used for alternative actions. It is on a transparent background with a border on hover and an active state.

Style

Every button for Allied Electronics can be built following the following criteria.

Maintain a 2pd border radius on all Allie Electronics Buttons. Minimum 36pd hieght with 16pd left and right margin and padding for every button. We use our brand font Verdana in ALL CAPS minimum size of 12pd.

Adding an Icon
When adding an icon to a button maitain a 6px padding between the verbiage and the icon. Continue to maintain the min height and padding to the left and right.
